About us

We Care For U is a DIPP recognised startup. With a vision to end poverty, forced labour, worker's exploitation, and human trafficking, Helpers Near Me is working towards creating an ecosystem of inclusive economic growth for unorganised blue-collar workers. Helpers Near Me uses a complex algorithm via its different digital platforms to enable blue-collar workers to find local employment opportunities. In turn, the platform helps you in finding & hiring nearby professionally verified blue-collar workers without the middlemen in between.
