Professional suite of services specifically designed to keep an old age person or an infant or an individual with a specific medical condition comfortable at home is known as home healthcare.

Home healthcare service suite includes geriatric cum old age assisted living, elderly companionship, baby care services, new born baby and mother care, long term medical care to improve the quality of life for bed ridden patients, post hospital rehabilitation, palliative cum end of life care nursing.

Carepur deal in the organized supply of elderly caretakers cum companions (Level 1), Trained Medical Attendants (Level 2), Qualified Nurses (Level 3) & ICU specialized Nurses (Level 4) on full time as well as visit basis , Physiotherapists (BPT/MPT), Emergency Medical officers ( BAMS/ MBBS) and Consultants on visit basis as per patient needs. Level 1 to level 4 support system can be requested for as low as 1 hr upto 24 hrs basis.

If your elderly/ loved one is in a bed ridden/ semi bed ridden condition, we can help your elderly live a better life with senior care assisted living services by providing basic caregiver and preventive healthcare suite which takes care of their routine medical tests and doctor appointments

Carepur can help you in providing trained Medical Attendant or Qualified Nurse based on Medical condition of your loved one. You can request starting with couple of hours upto 24 hrs of support system.
